About Me

My name is Thomas and I'm a 20 year old developer currently living in the Wilmington, NC area. I am enrolled at the University of North Carolina Wilmington pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science. I enjoy developing games and apps in my spare time.

Some examples of my work can be found here. | If you wish to connect with me, click here.


  • 1997 Born in Wilmington, North Carolina.
  • 2006 Created my first website dedicated to Club Penguin tips, cheats, and glitch exploits. This website would later reach 60,000 total unique visitors.
  • 2007 Created my first YouTube account as a marketing venture to supplement video material for my website.
  • 2009 Developed my first game with Adobe Actionscript 2.0. Published game on Kongregate.
  • 2011 My most popular game was published on this year. It was a remake of "The Impossible Game". Notably, an Actionscript 2.0 tutorial that I developed and published on Newgrounds attained the 'Daily 5th Place' medal. I also graduated middle school.
  • 2013 Created my first game using the Stencyl platform that reached the weekly front page on Stencyl.
  • 2014 Created my first Twitch.tv account that would later reach 1,000 followers. I also created my first Tumblr blog that would reach 40,000 total followers.
  • 2016 Began attending the University of North Carolina at Wilmington as a Computer Science major and started freelance work as a web designer.
